LINKS

Important (and some less important) links

Slack

Please make sure you are on the Phi Rho slack and are on the required channels. Most importantly, make sure you are on the officer announcements channel and are receiving notifications.

GroupMe

We don't use GroupMe for any super important information, but occassionally for less formal group messages. Feel free to add yourself to any of the groups from the email.

Shenans

Use this Google Sheet to sign up for all of our events. This is a great place to check what events are upcoming and see who is going to what. Make sure you are logged into your @umich.edu account.

Drive

Mostly for our officers, all of our important documents and folders are located in the Phi Rho Google Drive. Make sure you are logged into your @umich.edu account.
